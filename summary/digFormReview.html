Dig Form Review System

The Brief

The Dig Form Review System is a custom workflow solution that another developer and I developed to meet a specific need in our company. The system in place involved paper forms and emails.

Our task was to bring things from the stone age and increase overall efficiency.

The basic ideas was to provide a workflow to allow dig surveyors to submit forms from the field, and supervisors to review them.

Results:

Overall this system was a great success. With some adjustments from user feedback along the way, we were able to achieve our goals and add value to the project.
